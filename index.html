CBN-Regulated Bank Integration

We connect to your bank via Mono — a CBN-licensed open banking provider. We never see or store your banking credentials. Your login stays between you and your bank, always.

JWT Auth & Token Rotation

Every session uses short-lived JWT access tokens with automatic rotation. If a token is ever compromised, it expires within minutes — your account stays protected.

Ledger-Verified Transactions

Every transaction is cryptographically verified against a tamper-evident ledger. Your financial records are immutable — no one can alter historical data, not even us.

Read-Only Bank Access

Owoye only reads your transaction data. We have zero ability to initiate transfers, move money, or perform any action on your account. Your funds are always in your control.

Data Isolation by Business

Every Owoye business account is fully isolated at the database level. There is no shared data between companies. Your financial data is yours and only yours.

Sovereign Guarantee Enabled

Owoye operates under Nigeria's institutional banking framework with sovereign-level compliance obligations — the same standards that govern Tier-1 Nigerian banks.
